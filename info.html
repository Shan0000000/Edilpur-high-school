Basic Information

Edilpur High School is the official name of the academic institute, registered with the education authorities. The native or alternative name of the institute is ইদিলপুর উচ্চ বিদ্যালয়, often used in local Bengali language contexts.

The institute was established on 01 January, 1995, marking the beginning of its educational journey. It received official recognition on 01 January, 1996. It is categorized as a Secondary institution and operates under the board of Dhaka.

The institute is included in the Monthly Pay Order (MPO) system, determining government funding for teachers' salaries. The official registration number is 4207151202. For More Information visit sohopathi
